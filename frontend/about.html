About LexHub

Why We Built LexHub

Millions of Indians face legal questions every day — as tenants, employees, consumers, or first-time contract signers — but rarely know where to start. Licensed legal help isn't always nearby, affordable, or fast. LexHub exists to close that first gap: helping people understand the basics of Indian law in plain language, so they can walk into a lawyer's office, a police station, or a negotiation already informed.

What LexHub Does

💬

Legal Q&A

Answers legal questions in plain English, grounded in the IPC, CrPC, and the Constitution.

📄

Contract Analyzer

Reviews contracts clause by clause and flags risky or potentially illegal language.

📋

Document Summarizer

Turns dense legal documents — FIRs, notices, judgments — into simple summaries with next steps.

What LexHub Does NOT Do

  • Give legal advice specific to your case
  • Predict court outcomes or case results
  • Replace a licensed Advocate

Free Legal Aid in India

If you need real legal assistance, India's National Legal Services Authority (NALSA) and the District Legal Services Authorities (DLSA) provide free legal aid to eligible citizens, including women, children, SC/ST communities, industrial workmen, and persons with low income.

📞 NALSA Helpline: 15100 · nalsa.gov.in
